Ledger Wallet Client Webapp

Meant to be as light as possible. Altcoin-compatible.
  • Let's get started

For those times when you don't need a Swiss-army knife

A really simple application enabling you to sign transactions with your Ledger Wallet.

No sync, no local storage. We just query the required public information from the Blockchain.

As secure as the official application: this app just handles public information. Critical processes, of course, still run inside the Ledger Wallet's secure element!

Altcoin support. Maybe. We'll see how it goes.
